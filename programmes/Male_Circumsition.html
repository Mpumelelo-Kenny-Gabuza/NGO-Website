Male Circumcision Program

Promoting Health, Culture, and Education Through Safe Initiation

Safe Initiation

The Safe Initiation initiative ensures that traditional practices are carried out in a clean and medically supervised environment. Our focus is on the physical safety of initiates, the prevention of infections, and the promotion of post-initiation healing.

Traditional Practices

Respect for culture and tradition remains at the heart of this program. We work closely with traditional leaders, families, and health professionals to maintain the integrity of customs while aligning them with modern safety standards.

About the Program

This program was established to strengthen the link between traditional practices and public health. It provides education, support, and training to ensure that every participant experiences a safe, meaningful, and empowering initiation journey.

Community Support

We partner with local leaders and families to ensure every initiate receives proper guidance and community care.

Medical Assistance

Professional healthcare workers monitor safety procedures and provide medical assistance during and after initiation.

Knowledge & Awareness

Educational programs teach initiates about self-respect, hygiene, and discipline for a brighter and healthier future.

Leadership Building

Encouraging responsibility and mentorship to produce young leaders who contribute positively to their communities.
